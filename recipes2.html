Vegan Chocolate Brownies

  • Prep Time 20 minutes
  • Cook Time 18 minutes
  • Total Time 38 minutes

    • Ingredients

  • 175 g dark chocolate
  • 75 g coconut oil
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 cup + 1 tbsp plain flour
  • 30 g cocoa powder
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Melt the chocolate and coconut oil and set aside to cool until warm
  2. Whisk sugar and cream of tartar until stiff peaks form
  3. Sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder and salt and add to the chocolate mixture
  4. Transfer to the lined baking pan
  5. Bake in the pre-heated oven at 175 ℃ for 18 - 22 minutes

Peanut Butter Fudge

  • Prep Time 10 minutes
  • Cook Time 15 minutes
  • Total Time 25 minutes

    • Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cup granulated white sugar
  • 3 tbsp chocholate chips
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 1 - 2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Bring sugar and milk to boil for 2 minutes
  2. Add peanut butter and vanilla then remove from the heat
  3. Spread into prepared tray and refrigerate until firm
