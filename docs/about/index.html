    About Us

    Farinha S.A. was founded in 2018 in Canterbury. Since its creation we developed our recipes to help people with different allergies to eat delicious food.We are here to help, to guide, to inspire how to bring smiles in your family with our recipes. For us healthy foods are part of a happy life.

    Mission

    Create and produce safe food with quality products and unique flavor, promoting moments of pleasure.

    Goals

    By 2021 to be among the most remembered bakeries in its segment for the innovative recipes that we develop every day.

    Values

    Quality, safety and innovation
    Tradition and sustainability
    Zero waste products and respect for persons ethics
