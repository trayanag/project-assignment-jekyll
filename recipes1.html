Vegan Tahini Cookies

  • Prep Time 22 minutes
  • Cook Time 8 minutes
  • Total Time 30 minutes

    • Ingredients

  • ½ cup (100 g) tahini
  • 5 tbsp (95 g) maple syrup or honey
  • ½ tsp vanilla paste
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 cup + 1 tsp almond flour
  • 3 tsp white sesame seeds
  • 2 tsp black sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. In a bowl mix together all the ingredients except the sesame seeds
  2. Mix together the white and the black sesame seeds
  3. Shape one tablespoon of the cookie dough into a ball, roll it in the sesame seed mixture and place it onto a baking sheet
  4. Bake the cookies at 180 ℃ for about 8 minutes
  5. The tahini cookies keep well in a cool dry place in a closed container for about 1 week

Fruit Cookies

  • Prep Time 10 minutes
  • Cook Time 15 minutes
  • Total Time 25 minutes

    • Ingredients

  • 100 g soft butter
  • 50 g castar sugar
  • 50 g blueberries
  • pinch of salt
  • 150 wholenut or almond flour

Instructions

  1. Beat the butter and sugar together until fluffy. Stir in the flour and chopped blueberries to make a soft dough
  2. Divide the mixture in to 10-12 even sized balls
  3. Place them apart on the baking tray and flatten with the back of the tablespoon
  4. Cook for 12 minutes until browned
