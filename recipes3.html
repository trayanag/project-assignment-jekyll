Homemade Hot Chocolate

  • Prep Time 5 minutes
  • Cook Time 5 minutes
  • Total Time 10 minutes

    • Ingredients

  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • ½ cup cornstarch
  • ¾ cup (75 - 100 g) icing sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 200 g dark chocolate

Instructions

  1. Mix all hot chocolate mix ingredients together
  2. Add ¼ cup of the hot chocolate mix to a saucepan and ¾ cup of milk
  3. Cook the hot chocolate on medium-high heat with constant stirring until it thickens

Mulled Wine with cranberries

  • Prep Time 5 minutes
  • Cook Time 15 minutes
  • Total Time 20 minutes

    • Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups cranberry juice
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 2 pieces star anise
  • 3 cups dry red wine (Merlot or Cabernet Sauvignon)
  • ½ cup fresh cranberries

Instructions

  1. In a large saucepan combine the cranberry juice, sugar, cinnamon sticks and star anise
  2. Simmer for 15 minutes
  3. Stir in the wine and cranberries and bring back to a simmer. Serve warm.
